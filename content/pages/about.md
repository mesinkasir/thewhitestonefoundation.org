The Whitestone Foundations dba Whitestone Publications is a Colorado-registered 501(c)3 non-profit corporation.  It publishes two professional e-journals: *[The New Polis](http://www.thenewpolis.com)*, *[The Journal for Cultural and Religious Theory](http://www.jcrt.org)*, which are open access journals. It also sponsors webinars and seminars including *Critical Conversations* and *Difficult Discussions*. 

The Whitestone Foundation is engaged collaboratively with colleges, universities, collaborative research centers, and other organizations.
