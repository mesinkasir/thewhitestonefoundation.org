## Forging the Future of Critical Thought for The 21<sup>st</sup> Century
  
## About The Whitestone Foundation

The Whitestone Foundation, dba Whitestone Publications, is a Colorado-registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit corporation dedicated to open-access scholarship, critical inquiry, and public intellectual engagement.

Whitestone Publications publishes two professional open-access e-journals: [The New Polis](#) and [The Journal for Cultural and Religious Theory](https://jcrt.org). Together, these publications support a global network of scholars, writers, and public thinkers committed to rigorous research, cultural analysis, and critical reflection on the urgent questions shaping our world.

## Transformative Publications

[The Journal for Cultural and Religious Theory](https://jcrt.org) is one of the oldest and longest-running e-journals in the fields of religious studies, cultural studies, and critical theory. Since its founding, JCRT has provided a vital platform for innovative scholarship at the intersections of religion, culture, politics, philosophy, and theory.

The New Polis extends this mission by fostering timely conversations about society, democracy, culture, education, religion, and public life. Both journals are committed to making scholarship accessible beyond traditional academic boundaries through open-access publication.

## Focused on the Future

The Whitestone Foundation is committed to cultivating scholarship for the future. Through its journals, webinars, seminars, and public programs—including Critical Conversations and Difficult Discussions—Whitestone supports thoughtful dialogue across disciplines, institutions, and communities.

At the heart of Whitestone’s work is a commitment to building a global network of scholars dedicated to the evolution of scholarship, the practice of critical thinking, and the creation of intellectual resources for a changing world.
