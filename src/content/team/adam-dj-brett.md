Adam DJ Brett is Special Projects Editor for Whitestone Publications.

He earned a Ph.D. in religion at Syracuse University whose work explores how Christianity, colonialism, and culture have become interwoven in North America. Beyond academia, he works with the American Indian Law Alliance and Indigenous Values Initiative in support of Indigenous sovereignty, human rights, and social justice. When he is not reading or writing he is coding, playing dungeons & dragons, and watching lacrosse.
