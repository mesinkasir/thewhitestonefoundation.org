JC Smith (he/him) was born and raised in Dorsey, Mississippi, and joined the U.S. Army in 2008. After leaving the military, JC attended the University of Mississippi and received his BA in Philosophy with an emphasis in religious studies in 2022. JC received his first MA from DU in Religious Studies with a focus on lived religions. JC hopes to pursue a PhD in religious studies and apply his expertise to analyze contemporary religious folklore and myth-making within academia. JC lives in Denver with his wife and their three children. Outside of academics, JC is an avid alpinist who loves camping, working, and training dogs. His favorite thing to do is tell stories around an open fire.
